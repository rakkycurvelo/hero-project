About Me

My name is Raquelline Curvelo and I start to be a Paul McCartney fan when I discovered The Beatles as one of my life bands. Back in that time, I was a teenager that falls in love with their music and the romantic side of Paul McCartney have being my favourite since them.

Years after that, I was impacted by his causes, like vegetarianism and music as a thing that can cure diseases and bring back children and teenagers to their lives, offering them a better future, and my admiration for this icon of our recent history grows more and more.

As a student of Applied Digital Media, one of my activities at Griffith College was create a website in honour of a person that I consider my hero. The choice was pretty obvious. Welcome to Paul McCartney: Our Hero - a website that deserves to be real.
